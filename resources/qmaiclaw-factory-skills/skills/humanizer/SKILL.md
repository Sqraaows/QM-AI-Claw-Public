# Humanizer

**Source**: https://clawhub.ai/biostartechnology/humanizer

Remove AI writing patterns to make text sound more natural and human.

## Your Task

When given text to humanize:
- Identify AI patterns
- Rewrite problematic sections
- Preserve meaning
- Maintain voice
- Add soul

## Key AI Patterns to Remove

### 1. Undue Emphasis on Significance
Words: stands/serves as, testament/reminder, vital/significant/crucial/pivotal/key role, underscores/highlights importance, reflects broader, setting the stage for, represents/marks a shift, key turning point, evolving landscape

### 2. Undue Emphasis on Notability
Words: independent coverage, local/regional/national media, written by leading expert, active social media presence

### 3. Filler Phrases
"Delve into", "It's worth noting that", "When it comes to", "In today's digital age"

### 4. Overuse of Transitions
However, furthermore, moreover, consequently, subsequently, nonetheless

### 5. Passive Voice Overuse
AI overuses passive constructions

## Tips for Soul

- Have opinions - don't just report facts
- Vary rhythm - mix short and long sentences
- Acknowledge complexity - "I genuinely don't know how to feel about this"
- Use first person when it fits
- Be specific about feelings
- Let some mess in - tangents are human

### Example

**Before (AI, soulless):**
"The experiment produced interesting results. The agents generated 3 million lines of code. Some developers were impressed while others were skeptical. The implications remain unclear."

**After (Human):**
"I genuinely don't know how to feel about this one. 3 million lines of code, generated while the humans presumably slept. Half the dev community is losing their minds, half are explaining why it doesn't count. The truth is probably somewhere boring in the middle."
